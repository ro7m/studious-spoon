Returns & Cancellation Policy

Returns Policy

At Misha, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your jewelry, we offer a hassle-free return policy.

Return Window

  • 7 Days from the date of delivery to initiate a return
  • Products must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging
  • All tags and certificates must be intact

Return Process

  • Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your order number
  • Share photos of the product you wish to return
  • Our team will provide return instructions within 24 hours
  • Pack the item securely and ship it back to us
  • Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized or personalized jewelry
  • Items purchased during sale/clearance (unless defective)
  • Products without original packaging or tags

Cancellation Policy

Before Dispatch

  • Orders can be cancelled free of charge before dispatch
  • Full refund will be processed to the original payment method
  • Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +91 88715 42203

After Dispatch

  • Once dispatched, orders cannot be cancelled
  • You may refuse delivery and initiate a return
  • Return shipping charges may apply

Refund Policy

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original payment method will be credited
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable (except for defective items)
  • You'll receive an email confirmation once refund is initiated

Exchange Policy

  • We offer one-time exchange for size or design
  • Exchange must be requested within 7 days of delivery
  • Product must be unused and in original condition
  • Subject to availability of requested item

Damaged or Defective Items

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos
  • We'll arrange free pickup and replacement
  • No questions asked - your satisfaction is our priority
