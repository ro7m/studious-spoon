Jewelry Care Guide

×

Your Misha jewelry is crafted to last a lifetime with proper care. Follow these guidelines to maintain its brilliance and beauty.

Daily Care

  • Remove jewelry before showering, swimming, or exercising
  • Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals
  • Put jewelry on last when getting ready and remove it first
  • Wipe with a soft cloth after each wear to remove oils and dirt

Cleaning Your Silver

  • Use a soft, lint-free cloth to polish your jewelry regularly
  • For deeper cleaning, use warm water with mild soap
  • Dry thoroughly with a soft cloth immediately after washing
  • Never use paper towels or tissues as they may scratch

Storage Tips

  • Store each piece separately in a soft pouch or jewelry box
  • Keep in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Use anti-tarnish strips in your storage area
  • Avoid storing in the bathroom due to humidity

Preventing Tarnish

  • Wear your silver jewelry regularly‚Äînatural oils help prevent tarnish
  • Keep away from rubber, latex, and sulfur-containing materials
  • Remove before entering chlorinated water or hot tubs
  • Store with chalk or silica gel packets to absorb moisture

Professional Care

For heavily tarnished pieces or intricate designs, we recommend professional cleaning. Contact us for guidance on restoration services.
