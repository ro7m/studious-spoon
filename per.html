Jewelry Care Guide

Proper care can ensure your silver jewelry lasts a lifetime and maintains its brilliance.

General Care

  • Avoid contact with harsh chemicals like chlorine, bleach, and household cleaners
  • Apply lotions, perfumes, and hairspray before putting on your jewelry
  • Remove jewelry before swimming, showering, or exercising
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight

Cleaning Tips

Everyday Cleaning

  • Use a soft, lint-free cloth to gently polish your jewelry
  • Mix mild soap with warm water and clean with a soft brush
  • Rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a soft cloth
  • Clean after each wear to remove oils and residue

Deep Cleaning

  • Create a paste with baking soda and water
  • Gently rub the paste on tarnished areas with a soft cloth
  • Rinse thoroughly and dry completely
  • Use a silver polishing cloth for extra shine

Storage Tips

  • Store each piece separately to prevent scratching
  • Use anti-tarnish strips or pouches
  • Keep in airtight containers when not in use
  • Avoid storing in bathrooms due to humidity

What to Avoid

  • Chlorine and bleach can permanently damage silver
  • Saltwater can cause pitting and corrosion
  • Rubber contains sulfur which causes tarnishing
  • Excessive moisture accelerates tarnishing
Pro Tip: The more you wear your sterling silver jewelry, the less it tarnishes! The natural oils from your skin help maintain its shine.
