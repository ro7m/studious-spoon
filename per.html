Jewelry Care Guide

Your Misha jewelry is crafted to last a lifetime with proper care.

Daily Care

  • Remove before showering, swimming, or exercising
  • Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals
  • Wipe with a soft cloth after each wear

Cleaning

  • Use a soft, lint-free cloth regularly
  • For deep cleaning, use warm water with mild soap
  • Dry thoroughly immediately

Storage

  • Store separately in soft pouches
  • Keep in cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • Use anti-tarnish strips
